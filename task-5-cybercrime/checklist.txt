Stay Safe Online: Cybersecurity Checklist for Students

General Online Safety:
- Always check the URL of a website before entering any personal information
- Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages
- Never share your passwords or OTPs with anyone
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi networks
- Keep your device and apps updated regularly to prevent security vulnerabilities

UPI and Financial Safety:
- Always verify the receiver’s UPI ID or name before sending money
- Never approve unknown payment requests in UPI apps
- Do not scan QR codes from untrusted sources
- Banks or apps will never ask for your OTP or PIN, do not share it

Reporting Cybercrime:
- Report incidents at https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the helpline: 1930 (24x7)
